# Node Based Tire Wear

**Author:** Jesus Goose
**Source:** [BeamNG.com](https://www.beamng.com/resources/node-based-tire-wear.36502/)

Adds tire thermals and node-based tire wear to BeamNG. Tires now heat up under
load, lose grip when overheated, and physically wear down over time — node by
node. Hot/cold tire pressure differences, flat-spotting from lockups, and
graining are all simulated.

## Why this is great for CareerMP / RLS

- **Pit strategy matters.** Long stints chew through rubber, forcing real
  tire-management decisions on multi-lap or multi-hour races.
- **Tire choice matters.** Soft compounds heat fast and wear fast; hard
  compounds last longer but take longer to come up to temperature.
- **Cold-start realism.** First lap is always slower until the tires are in
  their working temperature window — perfect for cruise / career servers
  that simulate real driving conditions.
- **Skill ceiling.** Smooth drivers preserve tires; heavy abusers cook them.

## How install works in Content Manager

1. Click **Install**.
2. If you're not already signed into BeamNG.com from the main mod browser,
   a sign-in window will pop up. Log in with your BeamNG.com account.
3. Content Manager downloads the latest version directly from BeamNG.com
   using your logged-in cookies and copies the zip into the selected hosted
   server's `Resources/Client/` folder.
4. Players who join your BeamMP server will receive the mod automatically,
   exactly the same as any other client mod.

> **Note:** Because BeamNG.com requires a login to download mods, you must
> have an active BeamNG.com account. Content Manager never sees your
> password — sign-in happens in an Electron browser window owned entirely
> by you.
