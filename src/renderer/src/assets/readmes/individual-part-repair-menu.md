# Individual Part Repair Menu

**Author:** Jesus Goose
**Source:** [BeamNG.com](https://www.beamng.com/resources/individual-part-repair-menu.35878/)

Adds an in-game UI app that lets you repair individual vehicle parts instead
of "magically" repairing the entire car at once. Damaged a fender in a small
bump? Just fix the fender. Cooked a single tire? Replace just that tire.

## Why this is great for CareerMP / RLS

- **Granular cost control.** CareerMP charges money to repair vehicles —
  pay only for what you actually broke instead of a flat full-repair fee.
- **Roleplay-friendly.** Tow truck operators, mechanics, and body-shop
  servers can charge per-part for jobs they performed.
- **Race recovery.** Pit-stop one corner instead of resetting the whole car.
- **No more "oops, full repair" misclicks.** Pick exactly what to fix.

## How install works in Content Manager

1. Click **Install**.
2. If you're not already signed into BeamNG.com from the main mod browser,
   a sign-in window will pop up. Log in with your BeamNG.com account.
3. Content Manager downloads the latest version directly from BeamNG.com
   using your logged-in cookies and copies the zip into the selected hosted
   server's `Resources/Client/` folder.
4. Players who join your BeamMP server will receive the mod automatically,
   exactly the same as any other client mod.

> **Note:** Because BeamNG.com requires a login to download mods, you must
> have an active BeamNG.com account. Content Manager never sees your
> password — sign-in happens in an Electron browser window owned entirely
> by you.
